A very pleasant deck of oracle cards

“What if there was a fortune telling card deck that was very mean and pessimistic?” was the question that kickstarted this project. This 30-card deck lightheartedly pokes fun at both the allure of mysticism to solve life problems as well as the inner nihilist, pessimistic, and anxious thoughts of a person in their early twenties trying to navigate the world. Cards were riso printed.
