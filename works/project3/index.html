Wong Kar-Wai

Artist Publication

The publication design features Wong Kar-Wai who is a filmmaker from Hong Kong whose work focuses on the the passage of time, the state of constant change and the lack of permanence that comes with people who are always on the move. The publication features essay “Trapped in the Present: Time in the Films of “Wong Kar-Wai”” by Ewa Mazierska and Laura Ras- caroli. Images are stills from his films “In the Mood for Love” and “Happy Together.”
