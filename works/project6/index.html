How to talk to your mother

Letterpress Book

A letterpress-printed book about the anxiety and sadness that comes with calling your mother who lives halfway across the world. The book was made to be a series of index cards to emu- late the act of flipping through presentation notes, conveying the anxiety of having to think about what to say or saying the right thing The book serves as a time capsule for how a complex relationship is like in this specific moment in time, for the purpose of looking back on in the case that it changes and evolves with time.
